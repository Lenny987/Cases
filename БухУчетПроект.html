Case "Accounting Operations and Reporting" Part I

  1. On June 1, 2022, architectural designer Roman Hadid-Freskov decided to start his own business and invested ₽2,000,000 of his own funds by transferring money from his personal bank account to the company's bank account "Hadid-Freskov & Co".

    2. During June 2022, Roman had carried out the following transactions (all payments were made either from the bank account or using the corporate card linked to the bank account):

  2. June 1st - He made a prepayment of ₽40,000 for office rent for the month of June
  3. June 1st - He purchased office equipment for ₽200,000, but the payment was not made yet (so he bought it on credit)
  4. June 1st - He purchased office supplies for ₽30,000, but the payment was not made yet (so he bought it on credit; supplies shoud be better recorded as 'inventory')
  5. June 1st - Roman hired an assistant with a salary of ₽50,000 per month
  6. June 5th - Conducted a technical expertise of a mansion for the company "Realty" and issued an invoice for ₽280,000
  7. June 7th - Decided to contribute his own car which is worth ₽400,000 as an investment into the company
  8. June 9th - Purchased gasoline for ₽2,000, payment made (shoud be better recorded as 'inventory')
  9. June 12th - Invited a client for a business lunch and paid ₽4,000; they discussed preparation of a design project for a new factory, for which he plans to receive ₽500,000 as payment for the work when the contract would be signed
  10. June 16th - Developed a structural design project for "Landville" and issued an invoice for ₽240,000
  11. June 20th - Invited another potential client for a business lunch and paid ₽6,000, but it turned out that he would not be able to take on the work for this client
  12. June 23th - All office supplies purchased on June 1, 2022 were used
  13. June 25th - Used up all the gasoline purchased on June 9, 2022
  14. June 30th - The assistant's salary for June 2022 was accrued
  15. June 30th - The assistant's salary for June 2022 was paid
  16. June 30th - Paid the invoice for office equipment in the amount of ₽200,000
  17. June 30th - Paid the invoice for office supplies in the amount of ₽30,000
  18. June 30th - Received payment from the company "Realty" in the amount of ₽280,000
  19. June 30th - Withdrew ₽50,000 in cash from the corporate card for personal expenses
  20. June 30th - Received a confirmation from the landlord for the services provided for office rent for June 2022, in the amount of ₽40,000

Task for part I:

  1. Register the transactions in the General Journal (sheet "General Journal Part I"). Select the correct corresponding account from the dropdown list and enter the amount in the Debit cell
  2. Fill in and foot T-accounts (sheet 'General Ledger Part I') Transfer the operation index number from the General Ledger to the blue cells of respective T-accounts (only in the Debit and Credit columns); for operation #1 use the account "Cash" and the account "Shareholder's Capital"
  3. Prepare the Trial balance (sheet "Trial Balance Part I"). This register is generated automatically.
